About

Hey! I'm Matt Druce, an audio engineer and music producer currently located in Newcastle NSW.  I've been producing music for 5+ years.

 One thing I believe is that the space of a recording influences the sound. I've done work in great studios, but I've found that I prefer recording in everyday spaces - living rooms, garages, bathrooms -  you name it. Because of this I have acquired both gear and methods of recording that are portable and easy to take advantage of in any environment. Through me you will have the ability to record an awesome sounding project from your own home, jam space, or almost anywhere (which means no studio hire cost for you! - unless you want to work in a studio, which is totally cool, and in which case we can arrange something)
